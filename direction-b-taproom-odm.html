Your guide

I guessed what to study, over-prepared, and went in nervous.

When I studied for the Cicerone® exams, I wanted someone to show me exactly what to study, at what depth, and how to beat the tasting exam. It was frustrating, so after passing I built it myself. Since 2014, the majority of Certified Cicerones® have used my Beer Scholar course to pass.

Today I'm an Advanced Cicerone, a National BJCP beer judge, and a sensory and beer educator. I've spent over a decade turning the Cicerone syllabus into the fastest path to passing.
